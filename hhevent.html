Annual Charity Event 2025

Join us for a day of giving, hope, and community impact – December 15, 2025

About the Event

The Helping Hands Foundation is organizing its Annual Charity Event to raise funds and awareness for our education and healthcare initiatives. The event will feature inspiring speakers, success stories, community performances, and a donation drive to help us reach more lives.

Date: December 15, 2025
Time: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Venue: Hope Convention Center, Hope City, India

Event Schedule

Time Activity
10:00 AM Opening Ceremony
11:00 AM Keynote: Empowering Communities
12:00 PM Success Stories Presentation
1:00 PM Lunch Break
2:00 PM Live Performances
3:00 PM Fundraising Drive
4:00 PM Volunteer Awards & Closing

Featured Guests & Speakers

  • Anita Sharma Founder of Helping Hands Foundation
  • Dr. Ravi Kumar Public Health Advocate
  • Sneha Kapoor Women's Rights Activist
  • Local Youth Groups Cultural Dance & Music
