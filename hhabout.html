About Us

The Helping Hands Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the lives of underprivileged communities in India through education, healthcare, and empowerment programs. Since our inception, we have strived to bring dignity, hope, and opportunities to those who need it most.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate social workers, Helping Hands Foundation began with small community service projects in local villages. Over time, our initiatives expanded to multiple states, supported by volunteers, donors, and partners who believe in sustainable change.

Our journey started with just a handful of volunteers and has now grown into a wide-reaching movement of over 5,000 contributors working towards a common goal â€“ inclusive and equitable development for all.

Our Vision & Values

We envision a world where every individual, regardless of their background, has access to quality education, proper healthcare, and equal opportunities to succeed.

  • Compassion: Treating every life with empathy and dignity
  • Integrity: Transparency and honesty in our work
  • Inclusivity: Empowering marginalized voices
  • Sustainability: Creating long-term impact, not short-term fixes

Our Impact

  • 75,000+ meals served to families in need
  • 12,000+ children provided with school supplies and learning support
  • 250+ health camps across 7 states
  • 1,000+ women trained in self-sustaining livelihoods

With each passing year, we are expanding our reach and enhancing the quality of our programs to better serve communities across India.
